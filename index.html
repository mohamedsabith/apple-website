DESCRIPTION

The iPhone 13 Pro is Apple's smaller premium iPhone with a 6.1" screen size and for the first time in an iPhone, it comes with a 120Hz ProMotion display for super smooth scrolling. The list of innovations includes a more capable triple camera setup, with a wide, ultra-wide and zoom cameras, as well as a LiDAR scanner.

Apple iPhone 13

Apple iPhone 13 flaunts a 6.1-inch OLED Super Retina display with 1170x2532 pixels resolution and pixel density of 457 PPI. The phone is powered by hexa-core Apple A15 Bionic processor with 4GB RAM and 128GB storage. The device runs on iOS 15 operating system and the chipset features Apple GPU for graphics processing. The iPhone 13 packs dual rear cameras which includes 12MP primary sensor and 12MP ultrawide sensor. On the front, the phone has a 12MP wide camera for selfies, Face ID and FaceTime calling. The model gets a 3,265mAh battery that supports 20W fast charging via lightning port, and wireless charging. Other similar Apple mobiles you might be interested in includes the iPhone 12, iPhone 11, the iPhone 13 Pro, and the iPhone 12 Pro.

Apple Watch Series 5

Apple Watch Series 7, Apple Watch SE, and Apple Watch Series 3 have a water resistance rating of 50 meters under ISO standard 22810:2010. This means that they may be used for shallow-water activities like swimming in a pool or ocean. However, they should not be used for scuba diving, waterskiing, or other activities involving high-velocity water or submersion below shallow depth. Series 7 is also rated IP6X dust resistant.
